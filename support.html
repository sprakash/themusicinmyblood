Support

A story like The Music In My Blood is a keen reminder of the values that bind us all.

Here are the ways you can support our efforts.

Please note that your contribution is TAX-DEDUCTIBLE.
1. DONATE : A production such as ours incurs various costs to bring this experience to an audience. Any amount donated by you will help us in first and foremost, covering the cost of our venue, paying our team members, our rehearsal spaces, adding to our production value so we can correctly portray the environments where our story lives and reaching out to audiences who can experience this tale of inclusivity.
2. HOST A FUNDRAISER : Please reach out to us if you would like to host a fundraiser for the show.
You can contact us via email
3. BECOME A SPONSOR : We offer mentions on our active social media, in our show programs as well as special video content to promote your service or brand to our audience base. By April 2, the show would be played to approximate 700+ audience members in New Jersey and DC included.

With 14 shows in June 2018 at the 125 seater, you will have a chance to reach our wide audience base.
